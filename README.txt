JOGL World
---
A 3D world made using JOGL, in which you can walk and jump around in.
Controls: wasd to move, press q to enable autowalk feature and press e to enable/disable fog. Click and drag for freelook, letting go reverts back to original camera.

All textures, aside from the beehive, were from textures.com.
Skybox from:
http://www.custommapmakers.org/skyboxes.php

Beehive from:
https://www.filterforge.com/filters/12415.html
